About Us

Technology Adventure Society is a non-profit organisation under Technology Student's Gymkhana, IIT Kharagpur

We organise indoor adventure activities, outdoor treks and trips to indulge students in recreational activities. We aim to increase productivity of students by giving them a break from their hectic and monotonus academic life
